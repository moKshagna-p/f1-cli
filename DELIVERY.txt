DATA SOURCES:
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
All data from OpenF1 REST API (https://openf1.org):
  • /v1/sessions   - Get current F1 session
  • /v1/drivers    - Driver metadata and team colors
  • /v1/position   - Live driver positions and gaps
  • /v1/laps       - Lap times and sector data
  • /v1/pit        - Pit stop events
  • /v1/weather    - Weather conditions

No authentication required!
